Welcome to the Columbia University Badminton Club! We are the official badminton club of Columbia University, catering to Columbia students, faculty, staff, alumni, and affiliates. Our goal is to provide a welcoming environment for anyone interested in playing badminton. Whether you are picking up a racket for the first time or are a seasoned veteran of the sport, we would love to have you here!
