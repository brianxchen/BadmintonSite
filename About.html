About Us

The Columbia Badminton Club is for all who are interested in and love the sport of badminton. The club hosts two open gym practices per week, welcoming people of all skill levels. Every semester, the Badminton Team also competes in intercollegiate tournaments. The club’s mission is to cultivate and grow the badminton community on campus.  Navigate through the website for more information. 

Here are the most important things you need to know for Fall 2023:
Practice Location: Blue Gym @ Dodge (find directions  here )
Practice Times: Mondays & Fridays 7-10 PM

Eligibility: In order to be eligible to participate in badminton club an individual MUST:
 1. Be affiliated with Columbia University and have a Dodge Fitness Center membership.
2. Officially  register to join your club online. You must register again, even if you were active again last semester.
    -- Club Dues: $20 for undergraduates and $30 for graduate students and other Columbia affiliates    each semester regardless of when you join the club.
All skill levels are welcome. As long as you meet the above requirements, anyone of any skill level is welcome to join and play at our practices.

Badminton Fun

The club is not only about practice. We host an array of events throughout the year. In the past, we have organized events including our end-of-the-year formal during practice, club tournaments, and Valentine's day candygrams. Of course, there is also the weekly Plank Challenge. We have many more fun occasions planned :)
