Frequently Asked Questions

General

How do I register for the club?

Register on the Columbia Recreation website, linked here !

Are club dues refundable?

If you attended one or fewer practices, club dues can be refunded if you contact us within the first month of practices starting.

Are all skill levels welcome? 

Yes! We welcome all levels of players -- from complete beginners to seasoned veterans.

Practices

Where is practice held?

Practice is held in the Blue Gym of the Dodge Fitness Center. To find it, enter Dodge through the entrance adjacent to Havemeyer Hall and turn right. After going down the hallway, you should see a balcony. This balcony overlooks the Blue Gym. There are three staircases on your right that will take you down to the gym floor.

Can I borrow a racket?

Yes. The club provides both loaner rackets and birds. Dodge Gym also provides loaner rackets from the booth near the running track upstairs.

When is practice?

See spring schedule on the About page !

How do I set up nets?

If you are here during club hours, a member of the E-board should be on hand to assist. 

Are masks required?

According to the most recent Dodge guidelines, masks are recommended when not actively playing but not required.

Do we need to be there the whole practice or can we come whenever?

You can come whenever and stay as long (or as short) as you would like! There are no rules for when members arrive at and leave practice.

Do we need to attend every practice?

You do not need to attend every practice -- come whichever days you would like!

Will we be paired with someone or do we need to bring a partner?

Players usually come with a group of friends, but if you do not have anyone to play with, come to us and we will help you find a group to play with!
